🌐 What is Rentality?

Rentality is not just a Web3 car rental platform — it’s the first Mobility Layer for Real-World Assets (RWA) on-chain. We enable trustless peer-to-peer vehicle rentals, backed by real utility, real usage, and real revenue.

🚀 MVP Highlights

  • 80+ on-chain completed rentals
  • 2,000+ miles driven
  • Fiat and crypto payments fully integrated
  • On-chain user verification (KYC)
  • NFT-based car ownership
  • Dynamic smart contracts

🧩 What Makes Us Different

We’re not “Web3 Turo.” We solve what Web2 platforms can’t — eliminating brokers and centralized control, making mobility decentralized, transparent, and incentive-aligned.

📈 Vision

We’re building the Stripe of mobility in Web3 — the infrastructure layer that will power ownership, usage, and monetization of real-world vehicles across the decentralized economy.
